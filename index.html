Our Story

Two Guys Who Love Golf

Based right here in Innisfil, we're two passionate golfers who started Fairway Finds to make quality clubs accessible to everyone — from first-time players to seasoned low-handicappers. Every club we list has been personally inspected and graded so you know exactly what you're getting.

We serve buyers and sellers across Simcoe County and the GTA. Whether you're upgrading your bag or selling clubs you no longer play, we're here to help with honest advice and fair prices.
